Decode Politics: Why Rahul Gandhi gave up Wayanad, retained Rae Bareli, as Priyanka is set for poll debut

The Congress on Monday announced that Rahul Gandhi would retain his seat in Rae Bareli, Uttar Pradesh, and give up Wayanad in Kerala where Congress general secretary Priyanka Gandhi Vadra,his sister, will make her electoral debut. The announcement came after Congress’ top leadership on Monday held discussions at party president Mallikarjun Kharge’s residence “You are now going to have two MPs, I will keep visiting,”Rahul said in a message to the people of Wayanad.

“The people of Wayanad gave me support, the energy to fight at a very difficult time.” Addressing the media, Priyanka said, “I won’t let the people of Wayanad feel Rahul’s absence. I will work hard, try my best to make everyone happy in Wayanad, be a good representative.”
